A Valentine's Letter
⸻  ✦  ⸻
My dearest Sankina,

As I sit down to write this, my heart feels full. It's Valentine's Day, but loving you has never been limited to a date on the calendar. I feel it in the quiet parts of my day, in the way I smile randomly when I think of you, in the way my chest tightens when I miss you.

From the moment I met you, I knew you were different. Your smile caught my attention -- but it's your mind that keeps me captivated. The way you think, the way your thoughts flow, the depth behind your words… it turns me on in ways you probably don't even realise. And that laugh of yours? God. The way it escapes you so freely. It does something to me every single time.

Even though there's distance between us, you've managed to make yourself feel right here, in my thoughts, in my body, in my heart. I won't lie… I miss your touch deeply. I miss the idea of pulling you close, feeling you melt into me, hearing that playful tone in your voice when you're pretending to be innocent.

Sometimes I imagine the first time we finally close that distance. The way I'll look at you, slowly and intentionally. The way I'll hold your face before I kiss you. Not rushed, not hurried, just us. I think about tracing my hands along parts of you like I've been memorising you from afar this whole time. And knowing you… I already know you'll be shy and pretend to not want it as bad, but I know I can bring it out of you.

But beyond all of that, I need you to hear this clearly.

The time it takes for us to meet does not weaken what I am feeling. It strengthens it. Every day we wait is another day I choose you. I'm not going anywhere. You are not "too far." You are not "too delayed." You are worth the patience. Worth the anticipation. Worth every second. I hope I am too.

You calm me in ways you don't even know. Your presence feels safe. Your love feels intentional. And that combination is rare.

You are not just my Valentine today. You are my future experiences, my unanswered prayers being answered slowly, my deep desire wrapped in warmth.

I love your mind.
I love your laugh.
I love the fire in you.
And I love the softness only I get to see.

Happy Valentine's Day, my baby.

⸻  ✦  ⸻
Forever choosing you,
David Anamayi
